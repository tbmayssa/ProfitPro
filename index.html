About ProfitPro

ProfitPro is a comprehensive, professional-grade profit calculator designed for businesses, entrepreneurs, and financial professionals. Our mission is to make profit analysis simple, accurate, and accessible to everyone.

Key Features

  • Advanced profit and margin calculations
  • Support for VAT/Tax and discount calculations
  • Multi-currency support (8+ currencies)
  • Interactive charts and visualizations
  • Calculation history tracking
  • PDF report generation
  • Light and dark mode themes
  • Fully responsive design

How It Works

Simply enter your cost price, selling price, and quantity, along with any applicable taxes, discounts, and fees. ProfitPro will instantly calculate your total cost, revenue, profit, and profit margin. View your results in beautiful charts, download detailed reports, and keep track of all your calculations in the history section.
